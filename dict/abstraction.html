abstraction
n
1.
[c]
抽象概念；抽象
a general idea not based on any particular real person, thing or situation; the quality of being abstract
2.
[u]
出神；心神专注
the state of thinking deeply about sth and not paying attention to what is around you
3.
[u]
提取；抽取；分离
the action of removing sth from sth else; the process of being removed from sth else
