comparative
adj
1.
比较的；相比的
connected with studying things to find out how similar or different they are
2.
比较而言的；相对的
measured or judged by how similar or different it is to sth else
3.
（形容词或副词）比较级的（如 better、worse、slower 和 more difficult）
relating to adjectives or adverbs that express more in amount, degree or quality, for example
n
1.
（形容词或副词的）比较级形式
the form of an adjective or adverb that expresses more in amount, degree or quality
