circle
n
1.
圆；圆形
a completely round flat shape
2.
圆周；圆圈
the line that forms the edge of a circle
3.
圆形物；环状物；圈；环
a thing or a group of people or things shaped like a circle
4.
（剧院或电影院的）弧形楼座
an upper floor of a theatre or cinema/movie theater where the seats are arranged in curved rows
5.
（相同兴趣、职业等的人形成的）圈子，阶层，界
a group of people who are connected because they have the same interests, jobs, etc.
v
1.
[i]
（尤指在空中）盘旋，环行，转圈
to move in a circle, especially in the air
2.
[t]
~ sth
围绕…画圈；圈出；圈起
to draw a circle around sth
