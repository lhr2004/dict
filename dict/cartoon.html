cartoon
n
1.
（报刊中尤与政治或时事有关的）漫画，讽刺画
an amusing drawing in a newspaper or magazine, especially one about politics or events in the news
2.
动画片；卡通片
a film/movie made by photographing a series of gradually changing drawings or models, so that they look as if they are moving
3.
草图；底图
a drawing made by an artist as a preparation for a painting
