[c]

2.
[c]
示范；示范表演；演示
an act of showing or explaining how sth works or is done
3.
[c]
证明；证实；论证；说明
an act of giving proof or evidence for sth
4.
[c]
表达；表露；表现；显露
an act of showing a feeling or an opinion
