dedication
n
1.
[u]
~ (to sth)
献身；奉献
the hard work and effort that sb puts into an activity or purpose because they think it is important
2.
[c]
（建筑物等的）落成典礼
a ceremony that is held to show that a building or an object has a special purpose or is special to the memory of a particular person
3.
[c]
（书、音乐或演出前部的）献词
the words that are used at the beginning of a book, a piece of music, a performance, etc. to offer it to sb as a sign of thanks or respect
