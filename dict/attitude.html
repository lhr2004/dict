attitude
n
1.
[c]
~ (to/towards sb/sth)
态度；看法
the way that you think and feel about sb/sth; the way that you behave towards sb/sth that shows how you think and feel
2.
[u]
我行我素的做派
confident, sometimes aggressive behaviour that shows you do not care about other people's opinions and that you want to do things in an individual way
3.
[c]
姿势
