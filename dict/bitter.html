bitter
adj
1.
激烈而不愉快的；充满愤怒与仇恨的
very serious and unpleasant, with a lot of anger and hatred involved
2.
愤愤不平的
feeling angry and unhappy because you feel that you have been treated unfairly
3.
[ubn]
令人不快的；使人悲痛的；由痛苦引起的
making you feel very unhappy; caused by great unhappiness
4.
味苦的
having a strong, unpleasant taste; not sweet
5.
严寒的
extremely cold and unpleasant
n
1.
[u]
苦啤酒（在英国很受欢迎）
a type of beer with a dark colour and a strong bitter taste, that is very popular in Britain
2.
[uspv]
苦酒原汁（从植物中提取的苦酒精液体，可增加其他酒精饮料的味道）
a strong bitter alcoholic liquid that is made from plants and added to other alcoholic drinks to give flavour
