clerk
n
1.
职员；簿记员；文书
a person whose job is to keep the records or accounts in an office, shop/store, etc.
2.
（议会、法院等的）书记员
an official in charge of the records of a council, court, etc.
3.
旅馆服务台接待员
a person whose job is dealing with people arriving at or leaving a hotel
v
1.
[i]
当职员（或文书、书记员）
to work as a clerk
