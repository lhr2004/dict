ease

容易；轻易；不费劲

舒适；安逸；自在；无忧无虑

2.
[i]
（使）小心缓缓地移动
to move, or to move sb/sth, slowly and carefully
3.
[t]

to make sth easier
4.
[t]
~ (sth)

5.
[i]
~ (sth)
