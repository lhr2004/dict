den
n
1.
兽穴；兽窝
the hidden home of some types of wild animal
2.
窝点，窝子（尤指非法或邪恶活动秘密聚会处）
a place where people meet in secret, especially for some illegal or immoral activity
3.
休息室
a room in a house where people go to relax, watch television, etc.
4.
(informal)
书斋；书房
a room in a house where a person can work or study without being disturbed
5.
（儿童的）隐蔽玩耍处
a secret place, often made roughly with walls and a roof, where children play
