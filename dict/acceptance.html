acceptance
n
1.
[u]
接受（礼物、邀请、建议等）
the act of accepting a gift, an invitation, an offer, etc.
2.
[u]
同意；认可
the act of agreeing with sth and approving of it
3.
[u]
接纳，接受（为成员、会员等）
the process of allowing sb to join sth or be a member of a group
4.
[u]
无怨接受（逆境、困境等）；逆来顺受
willingness to accept an unpleasant or difficult situation
