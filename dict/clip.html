clip
n
1.
[c]
（金属或塑料的）夹子，回形针
a small metal or plastic object used for holding things together or in place
2.
[c]
首饰别针
a piece of jewellery that fastens to your clothes
3.
[sing]
剪短；修剪
the act of cutting sth to make it shorter
4.
[c]
电影片段
a short part of a film/movie that is shown separately
5.
[c]
(informal)
（用手）猛击，抽打
a quick hit with your hand
6.
[c]
子弹夹；弹匣
a set of bullets in a metal container that is placed in or attached to a gun for firing
v
1.
[t]
夹住；别住；被夹住
to fasten sth to sth else with a clip ; to be fastened with a clip
2.
[t]
剪（掉）；修剪
to cut sth with scissors or shears , in order to make it shorter or neater; to remove sth from somewhere by cutting it off
3.
[t]
碰撞（某物的边缘或侧面）
to hit the edge or side of sth
4.
[t]
~ sth (out of/from sth)
从…剪下
to cut sth out of sth else using scissors
