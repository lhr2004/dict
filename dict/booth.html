booth
n
1.
不受干扰的划定空间（如电话亭、投票间等）
a small confined place where you can do sth privately, for example make a telephone call, or vote
2.
临时货摊（或放映棚等）
a small tent or temporary structure at a market, an exhibition or a fairground , where you can buy things, get information or watch sth
3.
（餐馆中的）火车座，卡座
a place to sit in a restaurant which consists of two long seats with a table between them
