camper
n
1.
野营者；露营者；度假营营员
a person who spends a holiday/vacation living in a tent or at a holiday camp
2.
野营车（供旅行时居住）
a large vehicle designed for people to live and sleep in when they are travelling
3.
旅行拖车，宿营拖车（无发动机，由其他车拖动，多供度假时住宿用）
a road vehicle without an engine that is pulled by a car, designed for people to live and sleep in, especially when they are on holiday/vacation
