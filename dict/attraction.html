attraction
n
1.
[sing]
（尤指两性间的）爱慕，吸引
a feeling of liking sb, especially sexually
2.
[c]
向往的地方；有吸引力的事
an interesting or enjoyable place to go or thing to do
3.
[c]
有吸引力的特征（或品质、人）
a feature, quality or person that makes sth seem interesting and enjoyable, and worth having or doing
4.
[u]
吸引（力）
a force that pulls things towards each other
