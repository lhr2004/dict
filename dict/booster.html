booster
n
1.
助推器
a rocket that gives a spacecraft extra power when it leaves the earth, or that makes a missile go further
2.
（电器的）升压机，升压器
a device that gives extra power to a piece of electrical equipment
3.
加强剂量
an extra small amount of a drug that is given to increase the effect of one given earlier, for example to protect you from a disease for longer
4.
帮助（或激励、改善）…的事物
a thing that helps, encourages or improves sb/sth
5.
（尤指政治上的）支持者，拥护者
a person who gives their support to sb/sth, especially in politics
