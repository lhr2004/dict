comb
n
1.
[c]
梳子；篦子；压发梳
a flat piece of plastic or metal with a row of thin teeth along one side, used for making your hair neat; a smaller version of this worn by women in their hair to hold it in place or as a decoration
2.
[c]
梳理（头发）
the act of using a comb on your hair
3.
[c]
（公鸡的）鸡冠
the soft, red piece of flesh on the head of a male chicken
v
1.
[t]
~ sth
梳，梳理（头发）
to pull a comb through your hair in order to make it neat
2.
[t]
仔细搜索；搜寻
to search sth carefully in order to find sb/sth
3.
[t]
~ sth
梳理（羊毛、棉花等）
to make wool, cotton, etc. clean and straight using a special comb so that it can be used to make cloth
