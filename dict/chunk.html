chunk
n
1.
厚块；厚片；大块
a thick solid piece that has been cut or broken off sth
2.
(informal)
相当大的量
a fairly large amount of sth
3.
语块（话语组成部分）
a phrase or group of words which can be learnt as a unit by sb who is learning a language. Examples of chunks are ‘Can I have the bill, please?’ and ‘Pleased to meet you’.
