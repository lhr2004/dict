continuity
n
1.
[u]
连续性；持续性
the fact of not stopping or not changing
2.
[u]
（逻辑上的）连接，联结
a logical connection between the parts of sth, or between two things
3.
[u]
（电影或电视节目场景中服装、物体等的）一致性，衔接
the organization of a film/movie or television programme, especially making sure that people's clothes, objects, etc. are the same from one scene to the next
