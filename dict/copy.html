copy
n
1.
[c]
~ (of sth)
（尤指文件或艺术品的）复印件，副本，复制品
a thing that is made to be the same as sth else, especially a document or a work of art
2.
[c]
（书、报纸等的）一本，一册，一份
a single example of a book, newspaper, etc. of which many have been made
3.
[u]
（报刊等的）稿件；（可用于报纸文章或广告的）消息，信息
written material that is to be printed in a newspaper, magazine, etc.; news or information that can be used in a newspaper article or advertisement
4.
[c]
作业本；练习本
a book used by students for writing exercises, etc. in
v
1.
[t]
~ sth
复制；复印；仿造；临摹
to make sth that is exactly like sth else
2.
[t]
抄写；誊写
to write sth exactly as it is written somewhere else
3.
[t]
~ sb/sth
模仿；效法；仿效
to behave or do sth in the same way as sb else
4.
[i]
~ (from/off sb)
作弊；抄袭
to cheat in an exam, school work, etc. by writing what sb else has written and pretending it is your own work
