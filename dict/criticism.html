criticism
n
1.
[u]
批评；批判；责备；指责
the act of expressing disapproval of sb/sth and opinions about their faults or bad qualities; a statement showing disapproval
2.
[u]
（尤指对书、音乐等的）评论文章，评论
the work or activity of making fair, careful judgements about the good and bad qualities of sb/sth, especially books, music, etc.
