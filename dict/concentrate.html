concentrate
v
1.
[i]
集中（注意力）；聚精会神
to give all your attention to sth and not think about anything else
2.
[t]
~ sth + adv./prep.
使…集中（或集合、聚集）
to bring sth together in one place
3.
[t]
~ sth
（使）浓缩
to increase the strength of a substance by reducing its volume, for example by boiling it
n
1.
[c]
浓缩物
a substance that is made stronger because water or other substances have been removed
