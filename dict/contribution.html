contribution
n
1.
[c]
捐款；捐资
a sum of money that is given to a person or an organization in order to help pay for sth
2.
[c]
~ (to sth)
（给雇主或政府用作医疗保险、养老金等津贴的）定期缴款
a sum of money that you pay regularly to your employer or the government in order to pay for benefits such as health insurance, a pension, etc.
3.
[c]
贡献；促成作用
an action or a service that helps to cause or increase sth
4.
[c]
~ (to sth)
（书、杂志、广播、讨论等部分内容的）一则，一条，稿件
an item that forms part of a book, magazine, broadcast, discussion, etc.
5.
[u]
~ (to sth)
捐赠；捐助；（尤指）捐款
the act of giving sth, especially money, to help a person or an organization
