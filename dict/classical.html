classical
adj
1.
古典的；经典的；传统的
widely accepted and used for a long time; traditional in style or idea
2.
和古希腊与古罗马文化相关的；受古希腊与古罗马文化影响的
connected with or influenced by the culture of ancient Greece and Rome
3.
古典的
written in a Western musical tradition, usually using an established form (for example a symphony ) and not played on electronic instruments. Classical music is generally considered to be serious and to have a lasting value.
4.
古文的；文言的
ancient in its form and no longer used in a spoken form
5.
简洁优美的；朴实美观的
simple and attractive
