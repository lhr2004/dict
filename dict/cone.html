cone
n
1.
（实心或空心的）圆锥体
a solid or hollow object with a round flat base and sides that slope up to a point
2.
（实心或空心的）圆锥形物
a solid or hollow object that is shaped like a cone
3.
锥形交通标，锥形桶，路锥（常为红白色或红黄色的塑料锥形物，表示在维修道路等时禁止车辆通行）
a plastic object shaped like a cone and often red and white, or yellow, in colour, used on roads to show where vehicles are not allowed to go, for example while repairs are being done
4.
（盛冰淇淋的）锥形蛋卷筒
a piece of thin crisp biscuit shaped like a cone , which you can put ice cream in to eat it
5.
（松树或冷杉的）球果
the hard dry fruit of a pine or fir tree
v
