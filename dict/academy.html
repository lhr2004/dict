academy
n
1.
专科院校
a school or college for special training
2.
（艺术、文学、科学等的）研究院，学会
a type of official organization which aims to encourage and develop art, literature, science, etc.
3.
（苏格兰）中等学校，中学
a secondary school in Scotland
4.
（美国）私立学校
a private school in the US
5.
（英格兰不受地方政府直接管辖的）国立中等学校，国立中学
a secondary school in England which has some independence from local authority control
