cube
n
1.
立方体；立方形
a solid or hollow figure with six equal square sides
2.
立方形的东西（尤指食物）
a piece of sth, especially food, with six sides
3.
立方；三次幂
the number that you get when you multiply a number by itself twice
v
1.
[usupass]
~ sth
求…的立方
to multiply a number by itself twice
2.
~ sth
把（食物）切成小方块
to cut food into cubes
