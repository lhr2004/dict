counter
n
1.
（商店、银行等的）柜台
a long flat surface over which goods are sold or business is done in a shop/store, bank, etc.
2.
（厨房的）操作台
a flat surface in a kitchen for preparing food on
3.
（某些棋盘游戏的）筹码，棋子
a small disc used for playing or scoring in some board games
4.
（电子）计数器，计算器
an electronic device for counting sth
5.
[ususing]
~ (to sb/sth)
（对意见、态度等的）反对，反驳
a response to sb/sth that opposes their ideas, position, etc.
v
1.
[t]
~ (sb/sth) (with sth)
反驳；驳斥
to reply to sb by trying to prove that what they said is not true
2.
[t]
~ sth
抵制；抵消
to do sth to reduce or prevent the bad effects of sth
adv
1.
~ to sth
逆向地；相反地；反对地
in the opposite direction to sth; in opposition to sth
