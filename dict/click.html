click
v
1.
[i]
（使）发出咔哒声，咔哒（或咔嚓）响
to make or cause sth to make a short sharp sound
2.
[t]
（用鼠标或在触摸板上）点击，单击
to choose a particular function or item on a computer screen, etc., by pressing one of the buttons on a mouse or touch pad
3.
[i]
(informal)
被突然明白；豁然开朗
to suddenly become clear or understood
4.
[i]
(informal)
（与某人）顷刻成为朋友；受（某人）的欢迎
to become friends with sb at once; to become popular with sb
5.
[i]
(informal)
配合默契；运作协调
to work well together
n
1.
短而尖的声音；咔哒（或咔嚓）声
a short sharp sound
2.
（对计算机鼠标或触摸板的）点击，单击
the act of pressing the button on a computer mouse or touch pad
3.
（尤见于非洲南部某些语言的）吸气音
a speech sound made by pressing the tongue against the top of the mouth or the part of the mouth behind the upper front teeth, then releasing it quickly, causing air to be sucked in. Clicks are found especially in southern African languages.
