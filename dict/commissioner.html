commissioner
n
1.
（委员会的）委员，专员，特派员
a member of a commission (= an official group of people who are responsible for controlling sth or finding out about sth)
2.
警察局长；警长
the head of a particular police force in some countries
3.
（政府部门的）首长，长官
the head of a government department in some countries
4.
（美国体育协会的）总干事，主管人
(in the US) an official chosen by a sports association to control it
