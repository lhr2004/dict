Congress
n
1.
[c]
代表大会
a large formal meeting or series of meetings where representatives from different groups discuss ideas, make decisions, etc.
2.
[cspv]
国会；议会
the name of the group of people who are elected to make laws, in the US consisting of the Senate and the House of Representatives
3.
[cspv]
（用于某些国家的政党名称）国民大会
used in the names of political parties in some countries
