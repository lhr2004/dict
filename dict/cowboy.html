cowboy
n
1.
（美国西部的）牛仔，骑马牧人
a man who rides a horse and whose job is to take care of cattle in the western parts of the US
2.
（美国西部影片中的）牛仔
a man like this as a character in a film/movie about the American West
3.
(informal)
（尤指产品质量差或索价太高的）奸商
a dishonest person in business, especially sb who produces work of bad quality or charges too high a price
