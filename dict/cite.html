cite
v
1.
~ sth (as sth)
提及（原因）；举出（示例）；列举
to mention sth as a reason or an example, or in order to support what you are saying
2.
~ sth
引用；引述；援引
to speak or write the exact words from a book, an author, etc.
3.
~ sb (for sth)
传唤；传讯
to order sb to appear in court; to name sb officially in a legal case
4.
~ sb (for sth)
嘉奖；表彰；表扬
to mention sb officially or publicly because they deserve special praise
