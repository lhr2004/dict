history
n
1.
[u]
历史（指过去发生的所有事情）
all the events that happened in the past
2.
[sing]
（有关某个地方、主题等的）发展史，历史
the past events concerned in the development of a particular place, subject, etc.
3.
[u]
历史课；历史学
the study of past events as a subject at school or university
4.
[c]
历史（指历史记载或历史传说）
a written or spoken account of past events
5.
[sing]
~ (of sth)
（某人的）履历，经历；家族史；（某地的）沿革
a record of sth happening frequently in the past life of a person, family or place; the set of facts that are known about sb's past life
