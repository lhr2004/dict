2.
[c]

the contents of a cup
3.
[c]
杯（美国用作烹饪的计量单位）；量杯（金属或塑料量器）
a unit for measuring quantity used in cooking in the US; a metal or plastic container used to measure this quantity
4.
[c]
杯状物
a thing shaped like a cup
5.
[c]
奖杯；优胜杯
a gold or silver cup on a stem , often with two handles, that is given as a prize in a competition
6.
[sing]

a sports competition in which a cup is given as a prize
7.
[c]
