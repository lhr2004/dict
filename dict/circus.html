circus
n
1.
[c]
马戏团
a group of entertainers, sometimes with trained animals, who perform skilful or amusing acts in a show that travels around to different places
2.
[sing]
马戏表演（常在大帐篷里进行）
a show performed by circus entertainers, usually in a large tent called a big top
3.
[sing]
(informal)
引人注意的人（或事）；热闹场面
a group of people or an event that attracts a lot of attention
4.
[c]
圆形广场；环形交叉路口
a round open area in a town where several streets meet
5.
[c]
露天圆形竞技场
a place like a big round outdoor theatre for public games, races, etc.
