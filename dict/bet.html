bet
v
1.
[i]
下赌注（于）；用…打赌
to risk money on a race or an event by trying to predict the result
2.
[t]
(informal)
~ (that)…
敢说；八成儿
used to say that you are almost certain that sth is true or that sth will happen
n
1.
打赌；赌注
an arrangement to risk money, etc. on the result of a particular event; the money that you risk in this way
2.
(informal)
预计；估计
an opinion about what is likely to happen or to have happened
