accessory
n
1.
[usupl]
附件；配件；附属物
an extra piece of equipment that is useful but not essential or that can be added to sth else as a decoration
2.
[usupl]
（衣服的）配饰
a thing that you can wear or carry that matches your clothes, for example a belt or a bag
3.
同谋；从犯；帮凶
a person who helps sb to commit a crime or who knows about it and protects the person from the police
adj
1.
辅助的；副的
not the most important when compared to others
