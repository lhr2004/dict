compress
v
1.
[t]
（被）压紧，压缩
to press or squeeze sth together or into a smaller space; to be pressed or squeezed in this way
2.
[t]
~ sth (into sth)
精简；浓缩；压缩
to reduce sth and fit it into a smaller space or amount of time
3.
[t]
~ sth
压缩（文件等）
to make computer files, etc. smaller so that they use less space on a disk, etc.
n
1.
（止血、减痛等的）敷布，压布
a cloth that is pressed onto a part of the body to stop the loss of blood, reduce pain, etc.
