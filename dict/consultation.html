consultation
n
1.
[u]
咨询；商讨；磋商
the act of discussing sth with sb or with a group of people before making a decision about it
2.
[c]
商讨会；协商会
a formal meeting to discuss sth
3.
[c]
（向专家请教的）咨询会；（尤指）就诊
a meeting with an expert, especially a doctor, to get advice or treatment
4.
[u]
查找；查阅；查看
the act of looking for information in a book, etc.
