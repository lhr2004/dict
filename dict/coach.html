coach
n
1.
[c]
（体育运动的）教练
a person who trains a person or team in sport
2.
[c]
私人教师；（多指）考前辅导教师
a person who gives private lessons to sb, often to prepare them for an exam
3.
[c]
长途汽车；长途客车
a comfortable bus for carrying passengers over long distances
4.
[c]
（旧时载客的）四轮大马车
a large closed vehicle with four wheels, pulled by horses, used in the past for carrying passengers
5.
[u]
（客机的）经济舱
the cheapest seats in a plane
v
1.
（对体育运动、工作或技能进行）训练，培训，指导
to train sb to play a sport, to do a job better, or to improve a skill
2.
~ sb (in/for sth)
辅导（尤指为让学员通过考试）
to give a student extra teaching in a particular subject especially so that they will pass an exam
3.
~ sb (in/on sth)
指示；特殊指导；专门传授
to give sb special instructions for what they should do or say in a particular situation
