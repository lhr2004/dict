crib
n

（有围栏的）幼儿床
a small bed with high sides for a baby or young child
2.
（马、牛的）饲料槽
a long open box that horses and cows can eat from
3.
圣诞马槽（表现耶稣诞生的情景）
a model of the scene of Jesus Christ's birth, placed in churches and homes at Christmas
4.
(informal)
（考试作弊用的）夹带
written information such as answers to questions, often used dishonestly by students in tests
5.
住所；公寓；居所
the house, flat/apartment, etc. where sb lives
