benefit
n
1.
[u]
优势；益处；成效
an advantage that sth gives you; a helpful and useful effect that sth has
2.
[u]
福利费（政府对失业者、病人等提供的补助金）
money provided by the government to people who need financial help because they are unemployed, ill/sick, etc.
3.
[c]
（公司发的）福利，奖金；（保险公司发的）给付，保险金
an advantage that you get from a company in addition to the money that you earn; money from an insurance company
4.
[c]
慈善（或公益）活动
an event such as a performance, a dinner, etc., organized in order to raise money for a particular person or charity
v
1.
[t]
~ sb
对（某人）有用；使受益
to be useful to sb or improve their life in some way
2.
[i]
~ (from/by sth)
得益于；得利于
to be in a better position because of sth
