against
prep
1.
反对；与…相反；逆；违反
opposing or disagreeing with sb/sth
2.
对…不利
not to the advantage or favour of sb/sth
3.
紧靠；倚；碰；撞
close to, touching or hitting sb/sth
4.
以防
in order to prevent sth from happening or to reduce the damage caused by sth
5.
以…为背景；衬托
with sth in the background, as a contrast
6.
和…相比
used when you are comparing two things
