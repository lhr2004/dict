decorate
v
1.
[t]
~ sth (with sth)
装饰；装潢
to make sth look more attractive by putting things on it
2.
[i]
粉刷；油漆；糊墙纸
to put paint, wallpaper , etc. on the walls and ceilings of a room or house
3.
[t]
~ sth
点缀；装点
to be placed on sth in order to make it look more attractive
4.
[t]
~ sb (for sth)
授给（某人）勋章（或奖章）
to give sb a medal as a sign of respect for sth they have done
