damage
n
1.
[u]
~ (to sth)
（有形的）损坏，破坏，损失
physical harm caused to sth which makes it less attractive, useful or valuable
2.
[u]
~ (to sb/sth)
损害；伤害
harmful effects on sb/sth
3.
[pl]
（法院判定的）损害赔偿金
an amount of money that a court decides should be paid to sb by the person, company, etc. that has caused them harm or injury
v
1.
~ sth/sb
损害；伤害；毁坏；破坏
to harm or spoil sth/sb
