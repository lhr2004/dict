admission
n
1.
[u]
（机构、组织等的）准许加入，加入权，进入权
the act of accepting sb into an institution, organization, etc.; the right to enter a place or to join an institution or organization
2.
[c]
（尤指对过错、罪行的）承认，招认，招供
a statement in which sb admits that sth is true, especially sth wrong or bad that they have done
3.
[u]
入场费；门票费
the amount of money that you pay to go into a building or to an event
