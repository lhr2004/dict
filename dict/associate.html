associate
v
1.
[t]
~ sb/sth (with sb/sth)
联想；联系
to make a connection between people or things in your mind
2.
[i]
~ with sb
交往；（尤指）混在一起
to spend time with sb, especially a person or people that sb else does not approve of
3.
[t]
~ yourself with sth
表明支持；表示同意
to show that you support or agree with sth
adj
1.
非正式的；准的；副的
of a lower rank; having fewer rights in a particular profession or organization
2.
联合的；有关联的
joined to or connected with a profession or an organization
n
1.
同事；伙伴
a person that you work with, do business with or spend a lot of time with
2.
合伙人
an associate
3.
准学士（获得两年制高校学位）
a person who has an Associate's degree (= one that is given after completing two years of study at a junior college)
