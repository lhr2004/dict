creation
n
1.
[u]
创造；创建
the act or process of making sth that is new, or of causing sth to exist that did not exist before
2.
[c]
作品；创作
a thing that sb has made, especially sth that shows ability or imagination
3.
[sing]
（尤指《圣经》所述由上帝）创造天地
the making of the world, especially by God as described in the Bible
4.
[u]
世界；天地万物
the world and all the living things in it
