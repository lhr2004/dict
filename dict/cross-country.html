cross-country
adj
1.
越野（的）
across fields or open country rather than on roads or a track
2.
（尤指通过越野）横越全国（的）
from one part of a country to the other, especially not using main roads or routes
n
1.
[sing]
越野赛跑；越野滑雪比赛
a cross-country running or skiing race
2.
[u]
越野赛跑运动；越野滑雪运动
the sport of running or skiing across country
