automatic
adj
1.
自动的
having controls that work without needing a person to operate them
2.
无意识的；不假思索的
done or happening without thinking
3.
必然的；当然的
always happening as a result of a particular action or situation
n
1.
自动步枪
a gun that can fire bullets continuously as long as the trigger is pressed
2.
自动变速汽车；自动换挡汽车
a car with a system of gears that operates without direct action from the driver
